# 3. Product Overview

## 3.1 Why On-chain Trading Terminal?

### The "On-Chain Migration" is Real, but UX is Broken
- **"CEX Fatigue"**: Regulatory pressure and worsening user experiences on centralized exchanges.
- **Missed Opportunities**: Korean retail users often miss out on massive on-chain opportunities (Solana, Hyperliquid, Polymarket) due to CEX reliance.
- **Dynamic Liquidity**: As of Q3 2025, Koreans hold over $220B in foreign securities. The Asia-Pacific region drives over 60% of global derivatives volume.
