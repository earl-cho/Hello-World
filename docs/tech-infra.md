# Tech & Infra

## Session Keys
Our "One-Click" architecture allows you to sign once per session. Execute trades instantly without a popup for every single transaction, providing a CEX-level speed of execution.

## In-house Validators & RPC
We run our own dedicated validators and RPC nodes to ensure maximum performance.
*   **99.9% Uptime**: Reliable connectivity for uninterrupted trading.
*   **Low Latency**: Optimized data feeds and order execution.

## Brokerage & Revenue Model
Blackboard operates as a transparent brokerage layer.
### Builder Codes
Our system incentivizes ecosystem growth through "Builder Pins." Use specific codes to unlock tiered rewards and specialized UI features.
### Non-Custodial Revenue
We monetize through volume-based builder fees routed through our terminal, ensuring we never take custody of your primary capital.
