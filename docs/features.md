# Product Features: Perps & Predictions

Blackboard integrates the best-in-class decentralized primitives into a single terminal.

## 1. Perpetual Futures
Access deep liquidity for a wide range of assets without the need for an expiration date.

### Liquidity Aggregation (EdgeX & Hyperliquid)
Blackboard acts as a sophisticated router, connecting you to the most efficient liquidity pools on-chain.
*   **Order Book Centrality**: Unlike AMM-based DEXs, we prioritize CLOB (Central Limit Order Book) architectures, providing tight spreads and deep liquidity comparable to top-tier CEXs.
*   **HyperCore Performance**: Leverage the speed of Hyperliquid's native L1 for sub-second trade confirmation.

### tradFi Tradables (HIP-3)
Through the **HIP-3 (Hyperliquid Improvement Proposal 3)** framework, Blackboard is expanding the boundaries of on-chain finance.
*   **Synthetic Exposure**: Trade global indices, major stocks, and commodities as perpetual products.
*   **24/7 Access**: Trade traditional markets on-chain even when conventional stock exchanges are closed.

## 2. Prediction Markets
Bet on the outcome of real-world events with deterministic settlement and global liquidity.

### Polymarket & HIP-4 Integration
Blackboard provides a specialized interface for the world's largest prediction markets.
*   **Market Categories**: Politics, Sports, Finance, Crypto, and Pop Culture.
*   **Binary Outcomes**: Most markets are structured as "Yes" or "No" tokens that pay out $1.00 upon success.
*   **Transparent Settlement**: Outcomes are determined by decentralized oracles (e.g., UMA), ensuring that no single entity can manipulate the results.

### User Interfaces: General vs. PRO
We cater to both casual participants and professional "market makers."
*   **General UI**: Optimized for quick sentiment-based bets. Simple, mobile-friendly interface.
*   **PRO UI**: Designed for sophisticated builders. 
    *   **Advanced Charting**: View order book depth and historical price trends for prediction tokens.
    *   **Management Dashboard**: Track multiple bets and manage exposure with advanced filtering.
*   **Builder Ecosystem**: Pro users using builder codes like **Dreamcash** or **Based** unlock specialized features and revenue-sharing tiers.
