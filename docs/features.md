# Product Features

## Perpetual Futures
Trade the world's most liquid crypto assets and TradFi benchmarks with up to 50x leverage.

### Liquidity Routing
We aggregate liquidity from leading on-chain engines like **Hyperliquid** and **EdgeX**, ensuring you always get the best price and deepest order books.

### TradFi Tradables (HIP-3)
Through our integration with Hyperliquid's HIP-3 framework, you can trade global stocks, bonds, and commodities as on-chain perpetuals.

## Prediction Markets
Join the world's fastest-growing sentiment market. Bet on politics, sports, and cultural events with zero friction.

### Polymarket Integration
Seamlessly access Polymarket's deep liquidity directly from the Blackboard terminal.

### General vs. PRO UI
*   **General UI**: A simplified, intuitive view for casual participants.
*   **PRO UI**: A high-density interface for professional traders, featuring advanced charts and builder fee mechanisms.

### Builder Fees
Pro users and builders using specialized codes (e.g., *Dreamcash*, *Based*) can access unique revenue-sharing models through our brokerage system.
