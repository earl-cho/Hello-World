# Introduction

## What is Blackboard?
Blackboard is an all-in-one **On-Chain Terminal** that simplifies the complex world of decentralized finance (DeFi). We provide a seamless, CEX-like experience for trading perpetual futures and prediction markets, directly on-chain.

Whether you are a retail user in Korea or Japan looking for a simpler way to access global liquidity, or a crypto-native pro trader demanding high performance, Blackboard is your gateway.

## Vision & Mission
### Solving "CEX Fatigue"
Centralized exchanges (CEX) are increasingly burdened by regulatory pressure and deteriorating user experiences. Blackboard offers the transparency of on-chain trading without the typical complexity.

### Gateway to On-Chain Finance
We bring high-liquidity products (Hyperliquid, EdgeX, Polymarket) to regional users who have traditionally been locked out of these opportunities. Our mission is to make every asset—from crypto to global TradFi securities—tradable in a single, intuitive interface.
