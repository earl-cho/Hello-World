# 5. Business Model and Investment Terms

## 5.1 Brokerage Services - Builder Code Fees

We monetize trading volume without taking custody of user funds.
- **Perpetual Futures**: Builder code fees on transactions routed through the terminal.
- **Prediction Markets**: Revenue share on volume driven to Polymarket via our PRO UI.

## 5.2 Ecosystem & Wallet
- **Marketing Fees**: Revenue from token launchpads and vault services.
- **User Lock-in**: Rewards and incentives through our embedded wallet ecosystem to create a long-term user base.

## 5.3 Investment Terms & Structure

- **Target Raise**: $4,000,000 USD
- **Valuation Cap**: $20,000,000 USD (Post-Money)
- **Instrument**: SAFE (Simple Agreement for Future Equity)
- **Token Warrant**: Equity investors receive proportional token allocation if a native token is launched.
- **Exit Strategy**: M&A protection (Equity) or TGE liquidity (Tokens).
