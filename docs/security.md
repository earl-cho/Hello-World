# Security and Safety

Security is the core foundation of Blackboard. We utilize industry-leading technologies to ensure your assets and data are protected.

## Non-Custodial Principles
At Blackboard, we never take custody of your funds.
*   **Smart Contract Execution**: All trades and settlements are handled by audited smart contracts on decentralized L1/L2 networks.
*   **Permissionless Withdrawals**: You can withdraw your funds at any time, independently of the Blackboard interface, directly from the underlying protocol.

## MPC Key Management (Privy)
Our social login system uses **Multi-Party Computation (MPC)**.
*   **Distributed Keys**: Your private key is never stored in one place. It is divided into multiple shares distributed between your device and secure servers.
*   **No Central Point of Failure**: Even if one server is compromised, your assets remain secure as no single entity holds the full key.

## Smart Contract Audits
We only route liquidity to protocols that have undergone rigorous security audits.
*   **Hyperliquid**: Extensively audited by leading security firms, with a proven track record of handling billions in volume.
*   **Polymarket**: Built on Polygon and utilizes UMA's decentralized oracle for secure, manipulation-proof resolution.

## Risk Disclosure
While we prioritize security, on-chain trading involves inherent risks:
*   **Smart Contract Risk**: Although audited, new vulnerabilities can always emerge in underlying protocols.
*   **Market Volatility**: High leverage can lead to rapid liquidations during extreme market moves.
*   **Technology Risk**: Internet connectivity or network congestion can impact trade execution.

> [!CAUTION]
> Always trade with capital you can afford to lose. Ensure you understand the mechanics of leverage and liquidation before opening significant positions.
