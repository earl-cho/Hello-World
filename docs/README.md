# Blackboard On-Chain Terminal

**Gateway to On-Chain Finance**

Blackboard is an on-chain finance terminal that simplifies the complex world of decentralized finance, making it accessible to everyone, regardless of their experience level.

## Executive Summary

Blackboard is positioned as the primary gateway for millions of retail users, especially in the Korean and Japanese markets, to access global on-chain products. Built by DeSpread and a team of seasoned crypto-native experts, we bridge the gap between regional liquidity and global decentralized markets.

## Vision

Bridging the gap between regional liquidity and global Markets.

## Mission

**Trade Everything Anywhere**

We believe the mainstream is ready for unique crypto products like perpetuals and prediction markets. Our goal is to provide a seamless UX and leverage our extensive distribution networks to bring on-chain finance to the masses.
