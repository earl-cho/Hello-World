# Getting Started

## Frictionless Onboarding
Blackboard is designed to eliminate the technical friction traditionally associated with on-chain trading. No seed phrases, no complex gas management, no barriers.

### 1. Social Login via Privy
Our integration with **Privy** allows you to create a secure, non-custodial wallet using your existing social accounts.
*   **Supported Methods**: Google, Apple, Telegram, and E-mail.
*   **Embedded Security**: Upon login, a unique embedded wallet is created. This wallet uses multi-party computation (MPC) to ensure that your keys are never stored on a single server, providing both security and ease of use.
*   **No Seed Phrase Required**: You don't need to manage 12-word seed phrases manually. Access your funds as simply as you access any web application.

### 2. Funding Your Account
To start trading, you need to deposit assets into your Blackboard wallet.
*   **Supported Assets**: USDC, USDT (EVM-compatible chains).
*   **Deposit Process**: Simply copy your embedded wallet address from the dashboard and transfer funds from any CEX or external wallet.
*   **Fiat On-ramp (Upcoming)**: We are working with regional partners to provide direct fiat-to-crypto on-ramps for enhanced accessibility.

### 3. The Gasless Experience
One of Blackboard's signature features is **Gasless Trading**.
*   **Meta-Transactions**: We cover the network fees (gas) for your trades. You do not need to hold native tokens like ETH or SOL in your wallet just to pay for transaction costs.
*   **Uninterrupted Trading**: Focus purely on your strategy. Whether the network is congested or calm, your execution remains seamless.

### 4. Setting Up Your Workspace
Customization is key for professional traders.
*   **UI Layouts**: Switch between a "Lite" view for casual trading and a "Pro" view for advanced charting and depth analysis.
*   **One-Click Trading**: Enable **Session Keys** to authorize multiple trades with a single signature, enabling rapid-fire execution.

> [!IMPORTANT]
> **Your Funds, Your Control**: While Blackboard provides a seamless experience, it remains strictly **non-custodial**. We never have access to your private keys, and your funds remain yours at all times.
