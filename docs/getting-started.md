# Getting Started

## Frictionless Onboarding
Trading on-chain shouldn't require complex technical knowledge. Blackboard eliminates the "seed phrase" barrier using an embedded wallet.

### Social Login via Privy
Sign up instantly using your Google, Apple, or Telegram account. A secure, non-custodial wallet is created for you automatically.

*   **No Seed Phrases**: No need to install browser extensions or manage complex keys manually.
*   **Embedded Experience**: Focus on your trades, not your wallet setup.

## Your First Trade
1.  **Connect**: Log in via your preferred social method.
2.  **Deposit**: Transfer funds (USDC/USDT) to your embedded address.
3.  **Trade**: Select a market and execute your first trade.

> [!TIP]
> **Gasless Experience**: Blackboard supports gas-free transactions for a smooth trading experience. You don't need to hold native tokens (like ETH) just to pay for network fees.
