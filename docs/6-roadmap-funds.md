# 6. Use of Funds & 2026 Roadmap

## 6.1 Use of Funds

### Engineering: "The CEX Experience On-Chain" (40%)
- **Mobile-First "Lite" App**: A native-feeling mobile web interface.
- **Session-Key Infrastructure**: Server-signer architecture for trade execution without wallet popups.
- **Data Latency**: Direct-to-validator connections for faster order books and charts.

### Token Liquidity & Marketing Operations (40%)
- **"Gas Tank" Subsidies**: Subsidizing gas fees for the first 5 trades for new users.
- **GTM & UA Marketing**: Budget for mass social media campaigns, rewards, and affiliate programs.

### Growth & Legal Moat (20%)
- **Regulatory Framework**: Collaborating with top law firms (e.g., Kim & Chang) to solidify our status as a non-custodial interface.

## 2026 Roadmap
- **Q1**: Product development and Alpha testing.
- **Q2**: Public launch and aggressive marketing blitz.
- **Q2-Q3**: Expansion into TradFi asset trading (HIP-3).
- **Q4**: Market dominance and ecosystem expansion.
