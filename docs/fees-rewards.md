# Fees and Rewards

Blackboard offers a competitive fee structure and a robust incentive system for both retail and pro users.

## Fee Schedule
Blackboard utilizes a volume-based tier system. As your 30-day trading volume increases, your fees decrease.

| Tier | 30D Volume (USD) | Maker Fee | Taker Fee |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| Tier 1 | < $100,000 | 0.02% | 0.05% |
| Tier 2 | $100K - $1M | 0.01% | 0.04% |
| Tier 3 | $1M - $10M | 0.00% | 0.03% |
| VIP | > $10M | -0.01% (Rebate) | 0.02% |

### Maker Rebates
Professional market makers can earn **Maker Rebates** by providing liquidity to the order book. When your limit order is filled by another trader, you receive a percentage of the trade as a reward.

## Referral Program
Grow the Blackboard community and earn passive income.
*   **Earn up to 40%**: Receive a percentage of the trading fees generated by users who sign up using your referral link.
*   **Lifetime Rewards**: Commission is paid out in real-time and continues as long as your referrals keep trading.

## Builder Incentives (Dreamcash, Based)
Blackboard is built on a "Builder-First" model.
*   **Revenue Share**: Builders who deploy specialized UIs or communities using Blackboard's infrastructure (e.g., using codes like *Dreamcash* or *Based*) receive a structured share of the volume driven through their specific entry points.
*   **Ecosystem Rewards**: Participate in seasonal trading competitions and leaderboard challenges to win exclusive prizes and token allocations.
