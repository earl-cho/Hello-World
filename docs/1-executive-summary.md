# 1. Executive Summary

Blackboard is an on-chain finance terminal positioned as a **"Gateway to On-Chain Finance"**. We make it easy for anyone to use these products, regardless of experience level.

### Market Opportunity
Most Korean & Japanese retail users don't have access to on-chain products. Blackboard is built by **DeSpread** (a leading GTM & marketing company in Korea) and experienced team members who grew the crypto industry in Korea.

### Strategic Positioning
- **Gateway for Millions**: Expediting adoption of global on-chain finance.
- **Killer Apps Ready**: Web3 infra, regulation, and "Killer Apps" are finally ready for mass adoption.
- **Agile GTM**: Purely focusing on Go-to-Market to adapt to rapidly changing crypto market conditions.
